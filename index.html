Add Chase Offers

Bookmarklet that walks the Chase Offers grid and adds every available offer.

Add Chase Offers
Click does nothing. Drag the green button onto your bookmarks bar to install.

Use

  1. Log into Chase, open your card's Offers page (the grid of tiles).
  2. Click the bookmark.
  3. Open DevTools Console. When it logs added all!, every available offer has been added.

Source: github.com/julien-mo/add-chase-offers
