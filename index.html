Add Chase Offers

Bookmarklet that walks the Chase Offers grid and adds every available offer.

Add Chase Offers
Drag the button onto your bookmarks bar to install.

Use

  1. Log into Chase and open the Offers page (the grid of tiles).
  2. Click the bookmark. The page will bounce between each offer detail and the grid until every available offer has been added.

Source: github.com/julien-mo/add-chase-offers
